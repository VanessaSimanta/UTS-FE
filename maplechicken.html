Why You'll Love this Recipe

  • Super flavorful, versatile and easy to throw together.
  • This marinade also tastes great on steak, fish or shrimp and works as a salad dressing.
  • Probably already have all the ingredients sitting in your pantry!
  • Great for weekly meal prep served over rice, with roasted veggies or on a salad.

Tips and Tricks

  • Marinate the chicken for up to 12 hours for the most robust flavor!
  • Marinating the chicken too long can cause the chicken’s texture to break down resulting in mushy, tough chicken so make sure you don’t exceed 12 hours.
  • Make sure to add a little oil to the grill or grill pan so that the chicken doesn’t stick.
  • Reserve a little bit of marinade (before it touches the chicken) to brush on it while the chicken is grilling.

Maple Chicken

Moist chicken breasts marinated in a maple balsamic herb glaze, seasoned with fresh herbs, and grilled to perfection for a healthy weeknight meal!

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Servings: 4 Servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. To make the marinade, in a small bowl or jar, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, maple syrup, garlic, fresh thyme, salt, pepper and pinch of cayenne pepper until all combined.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in a shallow dish or a 1-gallon sealable plastic bag and add most of the marinade
  3. Place the chicken in the fridge and let it marinate for at least 1 hour or up to 12 hours. When ready to grill, remove the chicken from the dish or bag, shake off any excess and discard the leftover marinade.
  4. Heat a grill or grill pan on medium heat and brush the grill with oil to prevent the chicken from sticking. Grill the chicken 5 to 6 minutes on each side, or until cooked through and no longer pink
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
