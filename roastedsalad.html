Roasted Pasta's

This Roasted Pasta Salad loaded with fresh ingredients, all tossed together with a delicious lemon herb dressing!

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 30 mins

Servings: 8 Servings

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with foil or parchment paper for easy clean-up.
  2. Arrange the veggies on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place in the oven for about 20 minutes, until veggies are fork tender.
  3. While the veggies are roasting, bring your water to a boil, sprinkle in some salt, and cook the pasta according to the package directions.
  4. To make the dressing, whisk together all the ingredients in a jar or bowl until combined and smooth.
  5. When the veggies are done cooking, mix them together in a large bowl with the pasta salad and toss with the dressing. Stir in the fresh basil and season with additional salt and pepper, as needed. Enjoy!

Notes

This pasta salad will last up to 3 to 4 days in a sealed, airtight container in the fridge. Feel free to enjoy this warm or cold!
