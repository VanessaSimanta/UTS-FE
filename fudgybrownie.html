Tips for Making Fudgy Brownie

  • Don’t squeeze water out of zucchini! We need the the water from the zucchini for all that great moisture.
  • Mix the eggs and honey together really well. This is something I tested and it worked really well!
  • Don’t use granulated sugars.
  • Let the brownies cool completely.

Fudgy Brownies

These brownies are deliciously moist with a rich chocolate taste loaded with shredded zucchini and made healthier with zero butter or refined sugar!

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 40 mins

Servings: 16 Servings

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Using a box grater, shred one medium zucchini (you’ll need 1 1/2 cups).
  3. In a medium bowl, mix together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt, then set aside. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and honey really good for about a minute then mix in the coconut oil and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the dry ingredients in with the wet ingredients and mix with a spatula until just combined. Mix in the shredded zucchini and fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Pour brownie batter into a 9×9 baking dish lined with parchment paper or sprayed generously with nonstick spray.
  6. Bake the brownies in the oven for 30 to 32 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let the brownies cool completely before cutting. Enjoy!
