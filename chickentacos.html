Lime Chicken Tacos

This chicken tacos are simple, fresh and delicious making this the perfect EASY summer recipe!

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Servings: 5 Servings

Instructions

  1. Place chicken in a large ziplock bag or bowl.
  2. To make the marinade, whisk together lime juice, zest, olive oil, garlic, honey, and cilantro until combined and pour over top of chicken until completely coated. Cover and marinate chicken for about 4 hours to overnight.
  3. To make the coleslaw, combine cabbage, carrots, green onions and cilantro in a large bowl. In a smaller bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, honey and salt and toss together with slaw mixture.
  4. To make the avocado crema, combine the avocado, garlic, cilantro, yogurt, lime juice, sriracha and salt/pepper in a food processor and pulse until combined. With the motor running, slowly add the olive oil and water until smooth
  5. Pre-heat outdoor grill or grill pan on medium-high heat and add marinated chicken breasts. Sprinkle with a little salt and cook chicken about 4 to 5 minutes on each side until juices run clear. Transfer chicken to a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes before cutting. This keeps them juicy!
  6. To assemble the tacos, place chicken in a tortilla and top with slaw and a drizzle of the avocado crema.
